Fusebox

Event Production, Content Creation, Projection Mapping

Project Overview

Interactive Nature Studio maintains an ongoing collaborative relationship with Fusebox, contributing to 1-2 large-scale multi-event productions annually. Our comprehensive services include event production, technical drafting, fabrication, projection mapping, and content creation for both their regular Fusebox Festival events and their fundraising gala, FUSEBASH. The technical backbone of these productions leverages our expertise in Vectorworks, Unreal Engine, and TouchDesigner to create cohesive, immersive experiences.

Our primary goals for Fusebox productions are twofold: creating immersive environments that enhance visitor engagement with various performances, and developing compelling fundraising events that help Fusebox meet their financial objectives. The outcomes have consistently met or exceeded expectations, with successful events that not only provide memorable experiences for attendees but also generate the necessary funding to sustain Fusebox's innovative programming. What makes this partnership particularly valuable is the ongoing nature of our collaboration, allowing us to refine and evolve our approach with each successive event while maintaining a deep understanding of Fusebox's mission and aesthetic preferences.
