SXSW Experiential Installation

Interactive Experience

Project Overview

Interactive Nature Studio provided comprehensive technical and creative support for three high-end experiential installations at SXSW 2024. Our team handled technical drafting, 3D rendering, design assistance, project management, fabrication supervision, and installation across multiple venues. The project utilized a sophisticated technology stack including Vectorworks, Unreal Engine, Maya, Blender, Substance Painter, Substance Sampler, Photoshop, and Polycam, complemented by custom carpentry solutions.

The concept centered on creating bold, luxurious, and memorable experiences for SXSW attendees that would generate significant social media engagement. These installations were designed to establish Delta's presence at SXSW 2024 while educating visitors about Delta's innovative system for utilizing airline miles as currency for various purchases. Goals and Outcomes The primary goals were to create immersive spaces that would attract foot traffic, encourage social sharing, and effectively communicate Delta's brand message. The installations successfully achieved these objectives, with events running flawlessly across all venues and generating substantial digital engagement throughout SXSW. What made this project particularly remarkable was our small team's ability to coordinate complex tasks across three separate venues while maintaining exceptional quality standards. Our flexibility allowed us to accommodate numerous last-minute requests without compromising the overall vision or timeline.
