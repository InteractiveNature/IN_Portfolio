Technical Drafting & Production

Corporate event space design for Tech Conference

Project Overview

For this major tech conference, our team provided comprehensive technical drafting and production services to transform a conventional convention center into an engaging, dynamic corporate event space. The project involved detailed spatial planning, custom staging elements, and integrated audiovisual systems that balanced brand presence with functional requirements.

Our process began with detailed 3D modeling and technical drafting of the entire event space, allowing stakeholders to visualize and approve designs before implementation. During production, we managed all technical aspects of the event, from lighting and sound design to custom fabrication and installation of branded elements, ensuring a seamless experience for both presenters and attendees.
