Overview

Our technical event production services provide end-to-end solutions for creating exceptional event environments. From detailed technical drafting and spatial planning to on-site production management, we handle all aspects of transforming venues into precisely engineered spaces that bring your vision to life.

With our meticulous approach to technical design and execution, we ensure that every element of your event—lighting, sound, staging, video, and more—works together seamlessly to create a cohesive and impactful experience for your audience, regardless of event size or complexity.

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book technical production services?

+

For optimal results, we recommend engaging our technical production services at least 8-12 weeks before your event. This timeline allows for thorough planning, detailed drafting, and coordination with venues and vendors. For complex events or those in unique venues, additional lead time may be beneficial. However, we understand that timelines can be compressed, and we can accommodate more urgent needs when necessary.

What information do you need to create a production plan?

+

To develop a comprehensive production plan, we typically need: venue specifications and floor plans, event objectives and creative brief, estimated attendance numbers, program schedule and flow, technical requirements for presentations or performances, branding guidelines, and budget parameters. The more information you can provide, the more precise our planning will be, but we're skilled at asking the right questions to fill in any gaps.

Can you work with existing vendors or venue technical staff?

+

Yes, we regularly collaborate with existing vendors, in-house venue teams, and client-selected partners. Our role can range from providing complete technical management to offering supplemental support for specific aspects of your event. We pride ourselves on our collaborative approach and ability to integrate smoothly with other teams, ensuring all technical elements work together cohesively regardless of who provides specific services.
